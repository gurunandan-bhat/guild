about the publisher

The Film Critics Guild (FCG) is India’s first registered association of a geographically diverse cross-section of professional film critics who have come together to streamline the ethical and quality standards for film criticism in the country.

With India’s most credible critics — representing print, radio, broadcast, and the web — at its helm, the Guild is dedicated to maintaining the highest level of integrity amongst its members and evaluating works of cinema that stand apart in their craft.
