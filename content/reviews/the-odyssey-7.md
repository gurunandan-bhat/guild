In Interstellar, a mission to save humanity encompasses the universe, but the emotional core is a deeply moving father and daughter bond. In Inception, the grand, gravity-defying action may be a high-stakes blockbuster spectacle, but it is the deeply personal trauma of the protagonist that drives the narrative. A gritty beach evacuation in Dunkirk is held together by his silent, brooding moments. The backdrop of atomic fission in Oppenheimer is balanced with the psychological turmoil that plays within the man who catalyses it. In The Dark Knight, Gotham is refashioned as a sprawling, worldly canvas overrun with mobsters, corrupt law enforcers and vigilantes.
