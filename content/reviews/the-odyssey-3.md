How do you follow up Oppenheimer and its seven Oscars? If you're Christopher Nolan, you attempt to take on Homer and The Odyssey. The filmmaker's undertaking tackles several genres at one go. It's a literal Greek tragedy backed with adventure, drama, horror and a love story for the ages. All technical departments and the astonishing cast deliver an emotional adaptation that will likely uncover something new upon each viewing. The Odyssey is also the first feature shot entirely on IMAX cameras, and the result is definitely a thing of beauty.
