Main Vaapas Aaunga has its heart in the right place. A love story set against the backdrop of Partition and the horrors endured by millions has the ingredients for compelling cinema. But at over 160 minutes, Imtiaz Ali's Main Vaapas Aaunga takes an awfully long time to get where it wants to go.
