Any criticism against _Jana Nayagan_ hits a brick wall of an excuse, one that demands cinema be compromised for a superstar. Viewers are expected to overlook the film straying from narrative and form to cater to fans, because the film is a farewell gift made for the fans by a superstar who has built his career on such mindless commercial films. Hence, expecting anything out of the ordinary is dismissed as unwise. It becomes a matter of bargain.  But _Jana Nayagan_  can't just be that. It has another job. While it's a swansong for a superstar-actor, it also has to serve as a war cry for a new political leader. In the end, _Jana Nayagan_ is less a film and more fan service, and even more a campaign reel.
