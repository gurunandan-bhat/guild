The witty legal comedy Maamla Legal Hai was launched in March 2024. The Ravi Kishan-led series was a unique workplace show that based its cases and plotlines on real-life incidents. The Hindi series, created by Saurabh Khanna and Kunal Aneja, becomes more of a dramedy in its second season as its characters mature and deal with more weighty issues. The ensemble comedy also adds a few new faces to mix but Maamla Legal Hai continues to focus more on the gang at Patparganj District Court, whose internal squabbles become more entertaining than the cases they are fighting.
