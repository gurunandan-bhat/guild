Before you sit down to watch Musafir Cafe, do yourself a favour. Make sure you're actually free. Don't press play while waiting for a phone call, replying to messages or trying to fit in one more episode after a hectic day. Musafir Cafe needs your attention because it takes its own time. It lets its characters, conversations and emotions breathe, something we're honestly not used to anymore. We've become so used to rushing through everything, even watching shows at 2x speed, that slowing down almost feels unfamiliar. This is the kind of series you watch at the end of the day, when you're done with everything else and can simply let it unfold.
