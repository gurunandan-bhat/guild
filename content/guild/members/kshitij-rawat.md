Kshitij never consciously aspired to be a movie critic. He arrived there by instinct, and stayed by conviction. He has been reviewing and writing on films and television since 2017. He has developed a voice shaped as much by curiosity as by critique. Growing up in different parts of the country, he has been exposed to a variety of cultures, something that informs his work. He pays attention to how the story travels and connects. He is attentive to how stories translate and resonate. His writing has appeared in The Indian Express, WION, and Lifestyle Asia. Currently, he is working as an independent film and TV critic and writer.
