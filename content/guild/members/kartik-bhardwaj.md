Kartik Bhardwaj is Principal Correspondent (Entertainment), Film Critic and Journalist with The New Indian Express. He reviews Hindi films and web series along with the occasional International releases. Before his foray into film journalism, he worked on the news desks of The Times of India, The Indian Express, Hindustan Times and Mid-Day. He is currently based in Mumbai.
